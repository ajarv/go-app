Hey There

There are two types of addictions.

First type are when euphoria comes first and questioning later. e.g. You go to a party and you start drinking .. you feel good very good and you drink more. Later on next day you have a hangover and you question youself. - Why did I have to drink so much?

The second thpe is where questioning comes first and euphoria follows. e.g. Its 5 am , slightly cold outside and you are questioning yourself . Should I get out of bed and go for the Jog ? You finally go and at 7 am when you come back home you are feeling very good.

Avoid the first type of addictions. Adopt the second type.
